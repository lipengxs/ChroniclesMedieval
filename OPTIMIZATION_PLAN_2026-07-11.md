# Chronicles: Medieval SEO & content plan

## Baseline

GSC: 402 clicks, 35,981 impressions, 1.12% CTR, average position 10.06. Release-date and console queries have high demand but weak CTR: `chronicles medieval release date` has 3,025 impressions at 0.53% CTR.

## 0–30 days

- Add `WebSite`, `VideoGame` and visible FAQ structured data, plus a source-linked release/platform status page.
- State confirmed, unconfirmed and historical information separately; never infer console or multiplayer support.
- Improve titles and meta descriptions for release date, Xbox and PS5 intent.

## 31–60 days

- Turn official developer diaries and gameplay reveals into individual source-linked news pages.
- Expand battle-system hubs around formations, morale, retreats, command mode and culture-specific armies.

## 61–90 days and KPI

- Publish only when an official Steam/developer source changes, and log the source and review date.
- Target release/platform CTR above 1.5% and average position below six for the primary release-date page.
